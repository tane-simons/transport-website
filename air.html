Genairco Biplane

A full-size, Genairco biplane made of wood, metal, fabric, acrylic and rubber with a wood and fabric open cockpit, plane is painted silver with blue engine cowling. It is fitted with four cylinder Gipsy II air cooled in-lin engine with two-bladed wooden propeller.

1933
First Wright Bros. Aircraft Model

Aircraft model, Wright Flyer, representing first powered aircraft to fly built by Orville and Wilbur Wright and flown 17 December 1903, at Kitty Hawk, North Carolina, United States of America, model made by George Oliver Ingledew

1933
SE5A Aircraft Model

The SE5A was the best Allied fighter in the skies in 1917-18, and overall superior to the German Albatros series of fighters. It was not outclassed by even the newest German fighter, the Fokker D.V11, which first appeared in May 1918.
